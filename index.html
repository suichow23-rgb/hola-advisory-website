What We Do

We help SMEs decide their first Vietnam move.

Hola Advisory is not a heavy consulting firm. We help business owners understand whether Vietnam is worth exploring, what to watch out for, who may be useful to speak with, and what first step is practical.

Our advantage is operator experience. We have built in Vietnam through construction fit-out, co-working, retail distribution, and market-entry support for overseas founders. That means our advice is grounded in actual execution, not just desktop research.

If there is a real fit, we can also point clients toward company setup partners, virtual office, co-working, property sourcing, office or retail fit-out, franchise routes, business-for-sale opportunities, and local introductions.
